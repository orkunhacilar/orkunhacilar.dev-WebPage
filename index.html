Software Developer

A hardworking and disciplined software engineer student with strong computer and problem-solving skills.

The love of technology in me started by playing PlayStation at a young age. My connection with technology continued with the Industrial automation department in high school. We were working on arduino back then. By writing code via Visual Studio, we would insert these codes into small chips and make the light bulbs flash for certain seconds on the whiteboard. We would add buttons and make various applications. My passion for software increased while doing these works. Now I am studying software engineering at Izmir University of Economics. We have a training focused on Java. But I spend as much time as I can in different languages and fields and try to learn and solve this software world more.
